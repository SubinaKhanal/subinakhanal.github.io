I am a Research Assistant at the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University, Denmark, working with Professor Torben Bach Pedersen. Previously I was a graduate researcher in the ICNS laboratory Kyung Hee University, South Korea, under the supervision of Professor Eui-Nam Huh. For the past few years, I am working as a researcher in applied Machine Learning, with particular interests in Proactive Content Caching, Edge Computing, and Federated Learning.

Aside, I enjoy reading books, singing random songs, listening to wonderful music, dancing, and trying out new things; for now painting. Sometimes, I wander like the fireflies- tiny, but a meaningful creature, write half-done poems. Sometimes, I dive into the trivial mathematical proofs, curious about how things work, I play around with the concepts, and try my best not to explain myself-in an unconventional way. At least, I feel like that! I celebrate life- calm and simple- and, define myself with a single success, or a single failure. I enjoy little things in life, and find myself comfortable when closer to nature. I value human behavior, kindness, empathy, compassion, gratitude above anything. I also firmly believe in equal opportunity to access resources and making decision for both man and woman.
